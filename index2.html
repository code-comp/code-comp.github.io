About Us

Helping coders to gain problem solving and coding skills through coding competition.



As First-year Software Engineering students committed to learning new skills and knowledge, our mission in this project is to make the users gain critical thinking by being creative and focused on their goals. By solving and creating challenges, the users will discover ways of looking at problems from different aspects by finding solutions, thinking systematically and productively, and finding the most reasonable solution in short, logical reasoning.
